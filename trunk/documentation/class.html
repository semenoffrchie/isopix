isoPix

The PHP isometric engine, powered by GD2.

introduction

isoPix is a free PHP isometric engine, powered by GD2. isoPix handles isometric tiles and renders them to an image. isoPix provides advanced grouping/collection handlers, advanced co-ord systems, advanced rendering handlers and much more. isoPix is modular, meaning that you can easily use it and add to it, just remember to follow the CC licence, and send your changes back up the tree!
